Chronology

Year Milestone
1769 I was born.
1794 Took my sister and her children into my care.
1795 Was arrested and sentenced to 5 years for stealing a loaf of bread.
1800 My first escape attempt after losing contact with my sister.
1815 After additional years for attempting to escape, I am finally released.
1815 I meet Father Bienvenue, who offered me shelter and pardon.
1817 A miracle - a convict such as I opens a business.
1820 In another twist of fate, I become the mayor.
1823 I meet Fantine, a former employee who had been arrested by Javert. She begs me to take care of her child.
1823 I confess my identity and am arrested by Javert once again, but escape soon after. My goal was to rescue Cosette, Fantine's child.
